<p><i>Daily You</i> is an app made to store memories of the passing days. Log each day and look back on past moments. Works offline with support for external storage locations. <i>Daily You</i> is inspired by ptrLx's <a href="https://github.com/ptrLx/OneShot" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">OneShot</a>.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Take daily logs</li><li>Record your mood</li><li>Keep photo memories</li><li>Markdown support</li><li>Reminder notifications</li><li>Search your past logs</li></ul>
