<i>Daily You</i> is an app made to store memories of the passing days. Log every day and look back on past memories. All logs and photos are stored locally (the app has no internet access) and can be set to custom storage locations. <i>Daily You</i> is inspired by ptrLx's <i>OneShot</i>.

<b>Features:</b>
- Take daily logs
- Markdown support
- Keep photo memories
- Record your mood
- Search your past logs
- Material You design
- Set custom storage locations
- Photo gallery
- Log calendar
